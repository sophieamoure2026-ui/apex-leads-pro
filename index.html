Questions Contractors Actually Ask
Are these leads real people?
Yes. Every lead is sourced from a real public post (Craigslist, Nextdoor, Reddit, Facebook, permit filings) where the person actively requested the service. We don't buy lists or generate form fills. Each lead includes the source URL so you can verify it yourself.
What if a phone number doesn't work?
Email us within 7 days with the lead record. We replace it — no questions, no forms, no back-and-forth. Each pack includes free replacements (1–5 depending on tier). We stand behind the quality of every contact we deliver.
Who else gets these leads?
Nobody. Strictly one buyer per lead. We enforce a 90-day deduplication window — the same lead is never sold twice. Unlike Angi or HomeAdvisor where 4–6 contractors race on the same contact, your lead is yours alone.
How fast do leads arrive?
Leads are delivered within 2–24 hours of purchase depending on pack size. Our engine sweeps sources every 2 hours. Once your order is confirmed, the next available leads matching your vertical and city are queued for delivery to your email.
Can I choose my city and service type?
Yes. After purchase, email us your city (or metro area) and vertical. We match all leads to your specification. If you buy a larger pack, you can mix cities or sub-verticals. Zone Lock customers get 100% of all leads from their chosen zone.
